About the Game

DOOM Eternal is a fast-paced first-person shooter developed by id Software and published by Bethesda Softworks. As the Doom Slayer, players battle demonic hordes across Earth, Mars, and Hell using an arsenal of powerful weapons and brutal melee attacks. The game features intense combat, platforming elements, and an expanded story-driven campaign. With stunning graphics, fluid movement mechanics, and an aggressive combat system, DOOM Eternal delivers an adrenaline-fueled experience that pushes players to master the art of destruction.

DOOM Eternal System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4460 / AMD Ryzen 3 1200
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050 Ti / AMD Radeon R9 280
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-6700K / AMD Ryzen 7 1800X
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1080 / AMD Radeon RX Vega 56
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 50 GB available space (SSD recommended)

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
  • Available on PC via Steam & Bethesda Launcher
