About the Game

Asphalt Legends Unite is a high-octane racing experience where the world's best street racers come together to compete in the ultimate championship. Featuring intense multiplayer action, breathtaking tracks, and a vast selection of hypercars, players must prove their skills, customize their rides, and dominate the global leaderboards.

Asphalt Legends Unite

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 (10.0.16299) or later
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-3110M @ 2.40 GHz or AMD Ryzen 3 3300U @ 2.1 GHz
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Intel HD Graphics 4000, NVIDIA GeForce GT 420, or ATI Radeon HD 5450xw
  • Storage: 8 GB available space

Recommended:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or higher
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4460 @ 3.2 GHz or AMD Ryzen 5 3400G @ 3.7 GHz
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GT 1030 or AMD Radeon HD 5850
  • Storage: 6 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 3 & Xbox 360:

  • Requires respective console
  • Ensure sufficient storage space for installation and updates

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 60 GB of hard drive space
