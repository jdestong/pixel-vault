About the Game

Far Cry 4 is an open-world first-person shooter developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft. Set in the breathtaking Himalayan region of Kyrat, players take on the role of Ajay Ghale, a man caught in a violent civil war between a ruthless dictator and rebel forces. The game features a vast, explorable world filled with wildlife, intense gunfights, and various vehicles. With a rich single-player campaign, co-op multiplayer, and a deep crafting and progression system, Far Cry 4 delivers an action-packed adventure with endless possibilities.

Far Cry 4 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7/8/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-750 / AMD Phenom II X4 955
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 460 / AMD Radeon HD 5850
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 30 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7/8/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2400S / AMD FX-8350
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 680 / AMD Radeon R9 290X
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 30 GB available space

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 3 & PlayStation 4
  • Xbox 360 & Xbox One
  • Available on PC via Steam & Ubisoft Store
