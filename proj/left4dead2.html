About the Game

Left 4 Dead 2 is a cooperative first-person shooter developed and published by Valve. Set in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by infected hordes, players take control of one of four survivors, working together to escape through dangerous environments. The game features intense cooperative gameplay, a variety of weapons and melee combat, AI-driven enemy spawns, and multiple game modes including Campaign, Versus, Survival, and Scavenge. With its dynamic gameplay and replayability, Left 4 Dead 2 remains a fan-favorite among zombie survival shooters.

Left 4 Dead 2 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 / Vista / XP
  • Processor: Pentium 4 3.0GHz
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce 6600 / ATI Radeon X800
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 13 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7 / Vista / XP
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo 2.4GHz
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce 7600 / ATI Radeon X1600
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 13 GB available space

Console Availability

  • Xbox 360 (Backward Compatible on Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S)
  • Available on PC via Steam
