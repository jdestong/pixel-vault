About the Game

Silent Hill 2 Remake is a modern reimagining of the legendary psychological horror game, developed by Bloober Team and published by Konami. Players once again step into the role of James Sunderland, who ventures into the fog-shrouded town of Silent Hill after receiving a letter from his deceased wife. Featuring an over-the-shoulder camera, updated combat mechanics, and a deeply immersive atmosphere, the remake stays true to the haunting essence of the original while delivering a refreshed and terrifying experience. Silent Hill 2 Remake System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-8400 / AMD Ryzen 3 3300X
  • Memory: 12 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1080 / AMD Radeon RX 5700
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-8700K / AMD Ryzen 5 3600X
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2070 / AMD Radeon RX 6700 XT
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 50 GB available space (SSD recommended)

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 5
  • Available on PC via Steam
