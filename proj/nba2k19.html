About the Game

NBA 2K19 is a basketball simulation video game developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports. Released in September 2018, it is the 20th installment in the NBA 2K series and features Giannis Antetokounmpo on the standard edition cover, while the 20th Anniversary Edition features LeBron James.

NBA 2K19 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-530 / AMD FX-4100 (or better)
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GT 450 1GB / ATI Radeon HD 7770 1GB
  • Storage: 80 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7 / 8.1 / 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4430 / AMD FX-8370 (or better)
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 770 2GB / AMD Radeon R9 270 2GB
  • Storage: 80 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 3 & Xbox 360 & Nintendo:

  • PS4 / Xbox One: ~50 GB
  • Nintendo Switch: ~32 GB (digital version)

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 60 GB of hard drive space
