About the Game

TopSpin 2K25, released on April 26, 2024, marks the return of the acclaimed tennis video game series after a 13-year hiatus. Developed by Hangar 13 and published by 2K, the game is available on multiple platforms, including Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S.

TopSpin 2K25 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 or higher (64-bit)
  • Processor: 2 GHz or better
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: 1024 MB VRAM
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 15 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-3570K / AMD Ryzen 5 1400
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GTX 780 or better
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 15 GB available space

Console & Mobile Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
