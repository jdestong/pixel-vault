About the Game

NBA 2K20 is a basketball simulation video game developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports. It was released on September 6, 2019, for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch, and PC, with later releases on Google Stadia. The game is based on the National Basketball Association (NBA) and aims to provide a realistic basketball experience with enhanced gameplay mechanics, improved graphics, and new features.

NBA 2K20 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7/8.1/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-530 / AMD FX-4100 (or better)
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GT 450 / ATI Radeon HD 7770 (or better)
  • Storage: 80 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7/8.1/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4430 / AMD FX-8370 (or better)
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 770 / ATI Radeon R9 270 (or better)
  • Storage: 80 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 3 & Xbox 360:

  • PS4 / Xbox One: Around 50 GB of free space
  • Nintendo Switch: Around 32 GB (microSD recommended)

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Required for online modes, updates, and MyCareer progression
  • Offline play is available, but some features may be limited
