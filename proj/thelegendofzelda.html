About the Game

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is an action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo. Released on March 3, 2017, it is set in the open world of Hyrule, where players control Link, an amnesiac hero tasked with saving Princess Zelda and defeating Calamity Ganon. The game emphasizes exploration, experimentation, and nonlinear gameplay, allowing players to tackle objectives in any order.

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7, 8, or 10
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2500K or AMD equivalent
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 / AMD Radeon HD 7870
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 15 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-4770K or AMD equivalent
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics:NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 / AMD Radeon RX 480
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 20 GB available space

Console Availability

  • Nintendo Switch
  • Wii U
