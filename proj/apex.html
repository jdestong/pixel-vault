About the Game

Apex Legends is a free-to-play battle royale hero shooter developed by Respawn Entertainment and published by Electronic Arts. Featuring a diverse roster of Legends, each with unique abilities, the game delivers fast-paced, squad-based combat in dynamic environments. With fluid movement, strategic team play, and constant updates introducing new content, Apex Legends provides a thrilling and competitive experience. The game supports cross-platform play, allowing players to squad up with friends across different devices.

Apex Legends System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-6300 / AMD FX-4350
  • Memory: 6 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GT 640 / AMD Radeon HD 7730
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 56 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-3570K / AMD Ryzen 5 1400
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 / AMD Radeon R9 290
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 56 GB available space (SSD recommended)

Console & Mobile Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
  • Mobile (Apex Legends Mobile - Discontinued)
