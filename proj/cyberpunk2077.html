About the Game

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world action RPG developed by CD Projekt Red. Set in the futuristic metropolis of Night City, players take on the role of V, a mercenary seeking immortality in a world dominated by cybernetic enhancements, powerful corporations, and underground factions. The game features deep character customization, branching storylines, fast-paced combat, and a highly detailed environment. With stunning visuals, immersive gameplay, and continuous updates, Cyberpunk 2077 offers a gripping experience for fans of sci-fi and RPGs.

Cyberpunk 2077 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-3570K / AMD FX-8310
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 / AMD Radeon RX 470
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 70 GB available space (SSD recommended)

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-12700 / AMD Ryzen 7 7800X
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2060 / AMD Radeon RX 5700 XT
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 70 GB available space (SSD required)

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 5 (Next-Gen Edition)
  • Xbox Series X/S (Next-Gen Edition)
  • Available on PC via Steam, GOG, & Epic Games Store
