About the Game

Need for Speed: Most Wanted (2005) is an open-world racing game developed by EA Black Box and published by Electronic Arts. It is one of the most popular entries in the Need for Speed series, combining high-speed street racing with police chases and a compelling career mode.

SFS Most Wanten System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 2000/XP
  • CPU: 1.4 GHz Intel Pentium 4 or equivalent
  • RAM: 256 MB
  • GPU: 32 MB DirectX 9.0c compatible (NVIDIA GeForce 2 MX / ATI Radeon 7500 or better)
  • Storage: 3 GB free space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows XP
  • CPU: 2.4 GHz Intel Pentium 4 or equivalent
  • RAM: 512 MB
  • GPU: 64 MB DirectX 9.0c compatible (NVIDIA GeForce 6200 / ATI Radeon 9500 or better)
  • Storage: 3 GB free space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 2 (PS2):

  • Requires respective console
  • Ensure sufficient storage space for installation and updates

PlayStation 3 (PS3) (via backward compatibility)

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 60 GB of hard drive space
