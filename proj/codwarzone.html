About the Game

Call of Duty: Warzone is a free-to-play battle royale and multiplayer shooter developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activision. Featuring large-scale maps, realistic gunplay, and multiple game modes, Warzone offers a fast-paced and tactical combat experience. Players can drop into massive battles, engage in intense firefights, and utilize a variety of weapons, vehicles, and killstreaks. With constant updates, seasonal content, and cross-platform play, Warzone delivers an action-packed experience for fans of the Call of Duty franchise.

Call of Duty: Warzone System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-6100 / AMD Ryzen 3 1200
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 / AMD Radeon RX 470
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 175 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-6600K / AMD Ryzen 5 1400
  • Memory: 12 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 / AMD Radeon RX 580
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 175 GB available space (SSD recommended)

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
