About the Game

Gang Beasts is a hilarious multiplayer party brawler developed by Boneloaf and published by Double Fine Presents. Featuring gelatinous, physics-based characters, players engage in chaotic battles across a variety of hazardous environments. Whether you're punching, grabbing, or throwing opponents off ledges, the unpredictable ragdoll mechanics create wild and comedic moments. Gang Beasts supports both local and online multiplayer, making it a perfect game for parties or competitive fun with friends.

Gang Beasts System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 (64-bit) or later
  • Processor: 2nd Generation Intel Core i3 / AMD FX-4350
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 460 / AMD Radeon HD 7750
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 2 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2300 / AMD FX-6300
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 / AMD Radeon HD 7950
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 2 GB available space

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
