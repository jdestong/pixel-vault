About the Game

Little Nightmares is a dark, atmospheric puzzle-platformer developed by Tarsier Studios and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. Players take on the role of Six, a small girl trapped in the mysterious and disturbing world of The Maw, a nightmarish vessel filled with grotesque creatures. Blending stealth, platforming, and environmental puzzles, Little Nightmares delivers a hauntingly beautiful experience with eerie visuals, tense gameplay, and an unsettling narrative that unfolds without words. Escape the horrors of The Maw and uncover its dark secrets in this gripping adventure.

Little Nightmares System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-2115C / AMD A8-7600
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 460 / AMD Radeon HD 5770
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 10 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-3770 / AMD FX-8350
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 / AMD Radeon HD 7850
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 10 GB available space

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
  • Available on PC via Steam & Epic Games Store
