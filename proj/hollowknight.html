About the Game

Hollow Knight is an action-adventure Metroidvania game developed and published by Team Cherry. Players control a silent, nameless knight as they explore the ancient, ruined kingdom of Hallownest. The game is known for its hand-drawn art style, challenging gameplay, and atmospheric storytelling. Players encounter various enemies, friendly characters, and hidden secrets as they venture through interconnected areas. With fluid controls and intricate level design, Hollow Knight offers an immersive and rewarding experience.

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo E5200
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: GeForce 9800GTX+ (1GB)
  • Storage: 9 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: GeForce GTX 560+
  • Storage: 9 GB available space

Console Availability

  • Nintendo Switch
  • PlayStation 4
  • Xbox One
  • Available on PC via Steam, GOG
