About the Game

"League of Legends" (LoL) is a highly popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game by Riot Games. Players, divided into two teams of five, select from over 150 champions, each with unique abilities and roles, to battle on the map Summoner's Rift. The primary objective is to destroy the opposing team's Nexus. LoL combines skill, strategy, and teamwork, fostering a dynamic, competitive environment with a vibrant esports scene and a global player community. The game continually evolves with regular updates, ensuring fresh and engaging gameplay.

League of Legends Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-530 / AMD A6-3650
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVidia GeForce 9600GT / AMD Radeon HD 6570 / Intel HD 4600 Integrated Graphics
  • DirectX: Version 10.0
  • Storage: 12 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-Bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-3300 / AMD Ryzen 3 1200
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVidia GeForce GTX 560 / AMD Radeon HD
  • DirectX: Version 11.0
  • Storage: 12 GB available space
