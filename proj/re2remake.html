About the Game

Resident Evil 2 Remake is a survival horror game developed and published by Capcom. A modern reimagining of the 1998 classic, the game follows Leon S. Kennedy and Claire Redfield as they navigate the zombie-infested Raccoon City. With stunning RE Engine visuals, tense atmosphere, and reworked gameplay mechanics, Resident Evil 2 delivers a terrifying experience with improved combat, immersive environments, and an engaging narrative. Players must manage resources, solve puzzles, and fight against horrifying creatures to survive.

Resident Evil 2 Remake System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4460 / AMD FX-6300
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 / AMD Radeon RX 460
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 26 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-8700 / AMD Ryzen 5 3600
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1070 / AMD Radeon RX 5700
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 26 GB available space (SSD recommended)

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Available on PC via Steam
