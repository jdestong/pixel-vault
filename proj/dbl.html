About the Game

DRAGON BALL LEGENDS features action-packed anime action RPG gameplay. With intuitive fighting controls and simple card-based strategic gameplay, presented in stunning 3D animation & visuals, DRAGON BALL LEGENDS brings the anime to life to your mobile device!

DragonBall Legends Requirements


Mobile Requirements

Android

  • CPU: Snapdragon 625 Octa Core 2 GHz or equivalent
  • RAM: 3GB
  • Recommended system:Android 6.0 and later
  • Storage: At least 2 GB

IOS

  • iPhone 6S and later models.
  • Supported system:iOS 9 and later
  • Storage: At least 10 GB
