About the Game

Samurai Shodown (2019) revives the legendary fighting franchise with stunning visuals, intense sword-based combat, and a deep yet accessible battle system. Set in the late 18th century, the game stays true to its roots by emphasizing precise strikes, powerful counters, and high-risk, high-reward gameplay. Featuring a diverse cast of warriors, new mechanics like the Rage Explosion, Lightning Blade, and an advanced AI-driven Ghost system, Samurai Shodown delivers an authentic and thrilling samurai battle experience.

Samurai Shodown System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4460 or AMD FX-8320
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 770 or AMD Radeon R9 280
  • Storage: 35 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-6500 or AMD Ryzen 5 2600
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 or AMD Radeon RX 480
  • Storage: 35 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 35 GB of storage space for installation

PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, & Nintendo Switch:

  • Requires respective console
  • Optimized for higher resolution and performance
  • Minimum 35 GB of storage space
