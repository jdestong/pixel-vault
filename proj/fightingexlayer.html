About the Game

Fighting EX Layer is a fast-paced 2.5D fighting game developed by Arika, featuring classic characters from the Street Fighter EX series. The game introduces the unique Gougi System, allowing players to customize their fighting style with different power-ups and abilities during matches. With fluid animations, deep combat mechanics, and intense one-on-one battles, Fighting EX Layer delivers an exhilarating experience for both casual players and competitive fighting game enthusiasts. The game offers various modes, including online battles, training mode, and arcade challenges.

Fighting EX Layer System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7/8/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-4160 / AMD FX-4350
  • Memory: 6 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 / AMD Radeon R7 370
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 10 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4690 / AMD Ryzen 3 1200
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 / AMD Radeon RX 580
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 10 GB available space

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 4
  • PlayStation 5 (via backward compatibility)
  • Arcade
