About the Game

Mortal Kombat 1 reboots the iconic fighting franchise with a fresh story and revamped mechanics. The game introduces a new generation of fighters, including beloved characters and new challengers, all rendered in stunning graphics powered by Unreal Engine. The legendary Fatalities return, alongside new brutal finishing moves. Mortal Kombat 1 builds on the series' rich legacy with a deep story mode, exciting online modes, and visceral combat. The game also introduces the new "Kameo Fighters" feature, where players can summon classic characters to assist in battle, adding a new layer of strategy to the fight.

Mortal Kombat 1 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-6600K or AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 or AMD Radeon RX 580
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-9700K or AMD Ryzen 7 3700X
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2070 or AMD Radeon RX 5700 XT
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 5 & Xbox Series X/S:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 50 GB of storage space for installation

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 50 GB of hard drive space
