About the Game

Just Shapes & Beats is an action rhythm video game developed and published by the Canadian indie team Berzerk Studio. It was first released on May 31, 2018, for Windows and Nintendo Switch, and later for macOS, Linux, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S. In this game, players control a colored shape and dodge pink shapes that move to the beat of the background music. The gameplay consists of multiple levels based on individual music tracks, predominantly chiptune and electronic dance music (EDM) by artists like Nitro Fun, Danimal Cannon, Noisestorm, and Pegboard Nerds. Up to four players can control one small shape each, represented by different colors, and dodge various attacks in the form of beams, bouncing waves, pulsating shapes, and bouncing circles.

Just Shapes and Beats System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 or later
  • Processor: Intel Core i3 3.0 GHz 64-bit
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: DirectX 10-compatible graphics card with at least 256MB of video memory
  • Storage: 1 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5 3.0 GHz 64-bit
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: DirectX 11-compatible graphics card with at least 512MB of video memory
  • Storage: 1 GB available space

Console Availability

  • Nintendo Switch
  • PlayStation 4
  • PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One
  • Xbox Series X/S
