About the Game

Rugby 25, released on February 13, 2025, offers an immersive rugby gaming experience developed by Big Ant Studios and published by Nacon. The game features over 140 officially licensed international teams, including powerhouses like New Zealand, South Africa, England, France, and Australia, as well as more than 150 club teams across 11 competitions such as URC, English Premiership Rugby, French Top 14, MLR, and Super Rugby.

Rugby 25 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 or higher (64-bit)
  • Processor: 2 GHz or better
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: 1024 MB VRAM
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 15 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-3570K / AMD Ryzen 5 1400
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GTX 780 or better
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 15 GB available space

Console & Mobile Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
