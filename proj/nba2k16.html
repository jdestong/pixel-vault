About the Game

NBA 2K16 is a basketball simulation video game developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports. Released in September 2015, it is the 17th installment in the NBA 2K series.
The game features enhanced gameplay mechanics, improved player animations, and more realistic AI. One of its biggest highlights is the MyCareer mode, which includes a cinematic story mode directed by Spike Lee, following a player's journey from high school to the NBA.

NBA 2K16 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows Vista/7/8/8.1/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo or AMD Athlon X2 (2.4 GHz or better)
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: DirectX 10.1 compatible (512 MB)
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7/8/8.1/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5 or AMD FX (2.7 GHz or better)
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: DirectX 11 compatible (2 GB)
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 3 & Xbox 360:

  • Requires respective console
  • Ensure sufficient storage space for installation and updates

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 60 GB of hard drive space
