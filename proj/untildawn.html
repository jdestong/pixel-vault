About the Game

Until Dawn is an interactive horror adventure game developed by Supermassive Games and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. The game follows a group of eight friends who reunite at a remote mountain lodge, only to find themselves stalked by a mysterious threat. With a branching storyline shaped by player choices, Until Dawn features the "Butterfly Effect" system, where decisions impact character survival and the overall narrative. Combining stunning visuals, suspenseful storytelling, and multiple endings, the game delivers a cinematic horror experience filled with twists and terrifying moments.
