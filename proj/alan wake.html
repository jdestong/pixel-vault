About the Game

Alan Wake is a psychological action-thriller developed by Remedy Entertainment and published by Microsoft Game Studios. Players take on the role of bestselling writer Alan Wake, who finds himself trapped in the mysterious town of Bright Falls, where his latest novel is coming to life in terrifying ways. Blending intense action with a deep, suspenseful narrative, Alan Wake features cinematic storytelling, light-based combat mechanics, and a haunting atmosphere that keeps players on edge. As Alan searches for his missing wife, he must battle shadowy enemies known as the "Taken" while uncovering the dark secrets of Bright Falls.

Alan Wake System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows XP SP2 / Vista / 7 (64-bit recommended)
  • Processor: Dual-Core CPU (2 GHz)
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce 8800GT / ATI Radeon HD 2900
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 8 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7 / 8 / 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Quad-Core CPU (2.66 GHz)
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 275 / AMD Radeon HD 4870
  • DirectX: Version 10
  • Storage: 8 GB available space

Console Availability

  • Xbox 360 (Backward Compatible on Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S)
  • Available on PC via Steam & Epic Games Store
  • Remastered version available on PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, and PC
