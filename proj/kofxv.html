About the Game

King of Fighters XV brings the legendary fighting franchise into the modern era with its fast-paced, 3v3 team-based combat and stunning visuals. The game introduces new mechanics, including the "Shatter Strike" and "Max Mode" systems, giving players more flexibility and depth in their strategies. Featuring a diverse roster of characters from the KOF universe, as well as brand-new fighters, King of Fighters XV offers players a truly dynamic and exhilarating fighting experience. With extensive online modes, a rich story, and smooth gameplay, it's a must-play for both fans and newcomers to the series.

King of Fighters XV System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4460 or AMD Ryzen 3 1200
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 or AMD Radeon RX 580
  • Storage: 40 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-6700 or AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1660 or AMD Radeon RX 590
  • Storage: 40 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 5 & Xbox Series X/S:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 40 GB of storage space for installation

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 40 GB of storage space for installation
