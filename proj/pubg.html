About the Game

PUBG: Battlegrounds is a battle royale shooter developed and published by PUBG Studios. Players parachute onto a vast open-world map, scavenge for weapons and supplies, and fight to be the last one standing. The game features realistic gunplay, strategic gameplay, and a variety of maps with different terrains and weather conditions. With both solo and squad-based modes, PUBG offers an intense and immersive survival experience, making it one of the most popular games in the battle royale genre.

PUBG: Battlegrounds System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4430 / AMD FX-6300
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 / AMD Radeon R7 370
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 40 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-6600K / AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 / AMD Radeon RX 580
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 50 GB available space (SSD recommended)

Console & Mobile Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Android & iOS (PUBG Mobile)
