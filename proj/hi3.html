About the Game

Honkai Impact 3rd is a sci-fi adventure action game developed by HoYoverse. 3D cel-shaded graphics, dynamic combat with free-jumping mechanics, infinite combo, ultra-tight controls... Experience next-gen real-time action! An original tale told across media, immersive stage events, star-studded voice cast... Become part of the legend! While the crisis on Earth has momentarily abated, a new journey unfolds on Mars. Meet Valkyries with unique personalities and delve into the mysteries of Martian civilization together
