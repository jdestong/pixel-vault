About the Game

Just Dance is a popular rhythm game series developed and published by Ubisoft. The first game was released on November 17, 2009, and the series has since become a global phenomenon with annual releases and various spin-offs. In Just Dance, players follow on-screen dancers and mimic their moves to a variety of songs, ranging from classic hits to modern pop tracks. The game uses motion controllers or camera devices to track players' movements and score them based on their accuracy. The goal is to match the choreography as closely as possible to earn points and achieve high scores.

Just Dance System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 SP1 or Windows 8.1 or Windows 10 (64-bit versions)
  • Processor: Intel i5 750 @ 3.3 GHz or AMD Phenom II X4 955 @ 3.2 GHz
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 460 or AMD Radeon HD 5850 (1024MB VRAM)
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 15 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit version)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2400S @ 2.5 GHz or AMD FX-4320 @ 4 GHz
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 or AMD Radeon HD 7870 (2048MB VRAM)
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 15 GB available space
