About the Game

Guilty Gear Strive is the latest installment in the renowned Guilty Gear series, renowned for its fast-paced, technical combat and stunning anime-inspired visuals. The game introduces new mechanics, including the innovative "Roman Cancel" system, providing players with more control over their attacks and strategies. With a cast of unique characters, each with their own distinct fighting styles, Guilty Gear Strive offers both newcomers and veterans a thrilling experience. The game also features a deep story mode, online modes, and a robust training system that sets a new standard for modern fighting games.

Guilty Gear Strive System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 8 / 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4460 or AMD Ryzen 3 1200
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 or AMD Radeon RX 580
  • Storage: 20 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-6700 or AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 or AMD Radeon RX 580
  • Storage: 20 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 20 GB of storage space for installation

Xbox Series X/S & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 20 GB of storage space for installation
