About the Game

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is an action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. Released in 2013, it is set in the fictional state of San Andreas, inspired by Southern California. The game follows three protagonists—Michael De Santa, Franklin Clinton, and Trevor Philips—as they navigate a series of heists while dealing with personal conflicts and external threats. Players can freely explore the open world, engage in missions, and switch between the three characters. The game also features Grand Theft Auto Online, a multiplayer mode with various cooperative and competitive activities.

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 (build 1909 or above)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-4770 / AMD FX-9590
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1630 (4GB VRAM) / AMD Radeon RX 6400 (4GB VRAM)
  • Storage: 105 GB SSD
  • DirectX: Version 10

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 11
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-9600K / AMD Ryzen 5 3600
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics:NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3060 (8GB VRAM) / AMD Radeon RX 6600XT (8GB VRAM)
  • Storage: 105 GB DirectStorage-compatible drive
