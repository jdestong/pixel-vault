About the Game

"Mobile Legends: Bang Bang" (MLBB) is a popular mobile multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Moonton. In MLBB, two teams of five players each select from a roster of diverse heroes, each with unique skills and roles, to battle on the map known as the Land of Dawn. The main objective is to destroy the opposing team's base while defending their own. MLBB emphasizes fast-paced, strategic gameplay and teamwork, offering a competitive environment with a thriving esports scene. The game continually evolves with frequent updates and new hero additions, maintaining a vibrant and engaged player community worldwide.
