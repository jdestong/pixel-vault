About the Game

Brawlhalla is a free-to-play 2D platform fighting game developed by Blue Mammoth Games and published by Ubisoft. Featuring a diverse cast of Legends, players battle in fast-paced matches using unique weapons and abilities. With both online and local multiplayer, ranked modes, and frequent updates introducing new characters and events, Brawlhalla delivers an exciting and competitive experience for casual and hardcore players alike. The game supports cross-platform play, allowing players to battle against friends regardless of their gaming device.

Brawlhalla System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 (64-bit) or later
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo E8400
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Intel HD 3000
  • DirectX: Version 10
  • Storage: 800 MB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3 or better
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 460 or better
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 800 MB available space

Console & Mobile Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
  • iOS & Android
