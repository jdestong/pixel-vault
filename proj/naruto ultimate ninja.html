About the Game

Naruto Shippuden: Ultimate Ninja Storm 4 is the latest installment in the critically acclaimed Ultimate Ninja Storm series. Developed by Bandai Namco, it brings to life the epic battles and intense storylines from the Naruto Shippuden anime. Players can control a vast roster of ninjas from the Naruto universe, including iconic characters like Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha, and the mysterious Madara Uchiha. The game features beautiful graphics, fast-paced combat, and an engaging story mode that concludes the Naruto Shippuden saga. Ultimate Ninja Storm 4 also introduces dynamic 3v3 battles, allowing players to switch between characters during a fight and execute devastating combo attacks.

Naruto Shippuden: Ultimate Ninja Storm 4 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7/8/10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-4160 or AMD Athlon II X4 640
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 or AMD Radeon HD 7870
  • Storage: 40 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7/8/10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4690K or AMD Ryzen 5 1400
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 or AMD Radeon RX 580
  • Storage: 40 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 4:

  • Requires PlayStation 4 console
  • Minimum 40 GB of storage space for installation

Xbox One:

  • Requires Xbox One console
  • Minimum 40 GB of storage space for installation
