About the Game

Devil May Cry 5 is an action-adventure hack and slash game developed and published by Capcom. It was released on March 8, 2019, for PlayStation 4, Windows, and Xbox One, and later for PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, and Amazon Luna. The game is the sixth installment overall and the fifth mainline entry in the Devil May Cry series. The story follows returning protagonists Nero and Dante, along with a new character named V, as they are hired by a mysterious stranger to stop the Demon King Urizen. Each character has a unique playstyle, offering a variety of combat techniques and abilities.

Devil May Cry 5 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4460, AMD FX-6300, or better
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 760 or AMD Radeon R7 260x with 2GB Video RAM
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 35 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-3770, AMD FX-9590, or better
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 or AMD Radeon RX 480 with 8GB Video RAM
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 35 GB available space

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 4
  • PlayStation 5 (Special Edition)
  • Xbox One
  • Xbox Series X/S (Special Edition)
