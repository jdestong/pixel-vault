About the Game

Shadow Fight 2 is a thrilling blend of RPG and classic fighting gameplay, set in a world of shadows. Players take on the role of a warrior who must defeat powerful opponents using a variety of weapons, martial arts techniques, and magical abilities. The game features fluid animations, deep combat mechanics, and an engaging storyline that unfolds through intense battles. With numerous customization options, boss fights, and a compelling upgrade system, Shadow Fight 2 offers a unique and immersive fighting experience.

Shadow Fight 2 System Requirements

Mobile Requirements

Android:

  • OS: Android 4.1 and up
  • Processor: 1 GHz or higher
  • Memory: 1 GB RAM
  • Storage: 150 MB available space

iOS:

  • OS: iOS 10.0 or later
  • Compatible with iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch
  • Storage: 200 MB available space

PC Requirements (via Emulator)

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7/8/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel or AMD Processor (Dual Core)
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Integrated GPU or better
  • Storage: 500 MB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel i3 or better
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GT 710 or equivalent
  • Storage: 500 MB available space
