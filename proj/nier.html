About the Game

NieR: Automata is an action role-playing game developed by PlatinumGames and published by Square Enix. Released in 2017, it is set in a dystopian future where androids battle alien-created machines to reclaim Earth. The story follows androids 2B, 9S, and A2, exploring themes of existentialism, life, and war. The gameplay combines hack-and-slash combat, role-playing elements, and even segments inspired by other genres like shoot-'em-ups and text adventures.

NieR: Automata System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7/8.1/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-2100 or AMD A8-6500
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 770 or AMD Radeon R9 270X
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7/8.1/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4670 or AMD equivalent
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics:NVIDIA GeForce GTX 980 or AMD Radeon R9 380X
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 4
  • PC
  • Xbox One
  • Nintendo Switch
