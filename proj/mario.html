About the Game

Super Mario Odyssey is a 3D platformer game developed and published by Nintendo. Players control Mario as he travels across various worlds on his hat-shaped airship, the Odyssey, to rescue Princess Peach from Bowser, who plans to marry her. The game introduces Mario's new companion, Cappy, a sentient hat that allows Mario to possess other characters and objects, adding a unique twist to gameplay. With vibrant worlds, creative level design, and engaging mechanics, Super Mario Odyssey offers an unforgettable adventure.
