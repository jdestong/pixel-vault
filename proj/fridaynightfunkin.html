About the Game

Friday Night Funkin’ (FNF) is a rhythm-based music game where players control a character named Boyfriend, competing in rap battles to win approval from his girlfriend’s strict father. The game includes eight story-driven chapters (with a recent addition called Week End 1), each featuring unique opponents like Daddy Dearest, Skid and Pump, and Pico. Players press arrow keys in sync with the beat to out-perform rivals across catchy songs. Three difficulty levels are available, including a punishing “Hard” mode, while the new Night difficulty offers remixed tracks for seasoned players. The story mode progresses through weekly challenges, with Week End 1 introducing a chaotic reunion between characters Darnell, Nene, and Pico. This update includes three intense battles and a climactic story twist. For practice, Freeplay mode lets players tackle individual songs.
