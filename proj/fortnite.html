About the Game

Fortnite is a massively popular battle royale game developed and published by Epic Games. Featuring fast-paced combat, a unique building mechanic, and vibrant graphics, players compete to be the last one standing in 100-player matches. With constant updates, crossover events, and multiple game modes—including Battle Royale, Zero Build, and Creative—Fortnite delivers an ever-evolving experience. The game supports cross-platform play, allowing players to team up with friends across different devices.

Fortnite System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-3225 / AMD Ryzen 3 2200G
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Intel HD 4000 / AMD Radeon Vega 8
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 30 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-7300U / AMD Ryzen 3 3300U
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 / AMD Radeon R9 280
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 30 GB available space

Console & Mobile Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
  • Android (via Epic Games App)
