About the Game

NBA 2K22 is a basketball simulation video game developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports. It was released on September 10, 2021, for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, Nintendo Switch, and PC.

NBA 2K22 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 64-bit, Windows 8.1 64-bit, or Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-2100 / AMD FX-4100
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GT 450 / AMD Radeon HD 7770
  • Storage: 110 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4430 / AMD FX-8370
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 770 / AMD Radeon R9 270
  • Storage: 110 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 3 & Xbox 360:

  • PlayStation 4 (PS4) – Approx. 100 GB
  • PlayStation 5 (PS5) – Approx. 115 GB
  • Xbox One – Approx. 100 GB
  • Xbox Series X/S – Approx. 115 GB
  • Nintendo Switch – Approx. 45 GB

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 60 GB of hard drive space
