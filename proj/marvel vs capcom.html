About the Game

Marvel vs. Capcom: Infinite brings together iconic characters from the Marvel and Capcom universes for intense 2v2 tag-team battles. The game features a cinematic story mode, stunning visuals, and an improved battle system with easy-to-learn mechanics for newcomers while maintaining depth for veterans. The introduction of the Infinity Stones adds strategic elements, allowing players to harness unique abilities in combat. With a diverse roster, dynamic combat, and engaging online multiplayer, Marvel vs. Capcom: Infinite delivers thrilling crossover action.

Marvel vs. Capcom: Infinite System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-4160 @ 3.60GHz
  • Memory: 6 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 480 or AMD Radeon HD 6970
  • Storage: 59 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7, 8, 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4690K @ 3.50GHz or AMD FX-9370
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 or AMD Radeon R7 370
  • Storage: 59 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 59 GB of storage space for installation
