About the Game

Monster Hunter Rise is an action role-playing game developed and published by Capcom. It was first released for the Nintendo Switch on March 26, 2021, and later for Windows on January 12, 2022. The game was also released for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S on January 20, 2023. In Monster Hunter Rise, players take on the role of a Hunter, tasked with slaying or capturing large monsters using a variety of weapons, tools, and environmental features. The game introduces new mechanics, such as the Wirebug, which allows players to grapple and swing across gaps or to higher locations, and the Palamute, a new animal companion that can be used to ride across the map or into battle.

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-4130 or Core i5-3470 or AMD FX-6100
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GT 1030 (DDR4) or AMD Radeon RX 550
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 23 GB available space

Recommended:
