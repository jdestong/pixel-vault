About the Game

Persona 4 Arena blends the world of the critically acclaimed Persona 4 with fast-paced 2D fighting mechanics. Developed by Arc System Works, this game features beloved characters from the Persona universe in thrilling one-on-one battles. Players can utilize each character's unique Persona abilities and fighting styles, resulting in dynamic and strategic combat. The game also continues the story from Persona 4, introducing new characters and expanding on the lore, while delivering a rich single-player experience alongside multiplayer modes. With its beautiful hand-drawn visuals and accessible yet deep fighting system, Persona 4 Arena appeals to both Persona fans and fighting game enthusiasts alike.

Persona 4 Arena System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-4160 or AMD FX-6300
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 460 or AMD Radeon HD 6850
  • Storage: 10 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-6500 or AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960 or AMD Radeon RX 560
  • Storage: 10 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 3:

  • Requires PlayStation 3 console
  • Minimum 10 GB of hard drive space

Xbox 360:

  • Requires Xbox 360 console
  • Minimum 10 GB of hard drive space
