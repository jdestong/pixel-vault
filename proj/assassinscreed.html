About the Game

Assassin's Creed: Brotherhood is an action-adventure game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft. It is the third major installment in the Assassin's Creed series and a direct sequel to Assassin's Creed II. The game was first released in November 2010 for PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360, and later for Microsoft Windows and OS X. The game follows the story of Ezio Auditore da Firenze, a legendary Master Assassin, as he continues his fight against the powerful Templar Order. The main story is set during the Italian Wars, spanning the years 1500–1507, and takes place in Rome. Ezio aims to rebuild the Assassin Brotherhood in Italy and liberate the city from the control of the Borgia family.

Assassin's Creed: Brotherhood System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows XP/Vista/7
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo 1.8 GHz or AMD Athlon X2 64 2.4GHz
  • Memory: 1.5 GB RAM
  • Graphics: 256 MB DirectX 9.0–compliant card with Shader Model 3.0 or higher (NVIDIA GeForce 7800 / ATI Radeon X1950)B
  • DirectX: DirectX 9.0c
  • Hard Drive: 8 GB free space
  • Sound: DirectX 9.0–compliant sound card

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows XP/Vista/7
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo E6700 2.6 GHz or AMD Athlon 64 X2 6000+ 3.0GHz or better
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: 512 MB DirectX 9.0–compliant card with Shader Model 3.0 or higher (NVIDIA GeForce 8800 GT / ATI Radeon HD 4700)
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Sound: 5.1 sound card

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 3
  • Xbox 360
  • PlayStation 4(via The Ezio Collection)
  • Xbox One(via The Ezio Collection)
  • PC
