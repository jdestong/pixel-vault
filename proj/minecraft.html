About the Game

Minecraft is a sandbox game developed by Mojang Studios, first released in 2011. It allows players to explore a blocky, procedurally generated 3D world, where they can mine resources, craft tools, build structures, and engage in combat. The game offers various modes, including Survival Mode, where players must gather resources and maintain health, and Creative Mode, which provides unlimited resources for building and exploration. Minecraft's versatility and creativity have made it one of the best-selling video games of all time.

Minecraft System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 or later, macOS, or Linux
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-3210 or AMD A8-7600
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • GPU: Intel HD Graphics 4000 or AMD Radeon R5
  • Storage: 1 GB

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 or later
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4690 or AMD equivalent
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics:NVIDIA NVIDIA GeForce 700 Series or AMD Radeon RX 200 Series
  • Storage: 4 GB available space
