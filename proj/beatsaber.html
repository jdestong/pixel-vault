About the Game

Beat Saber is a virtual reality rhythm game developed by Ján Ilavský, Vladimír Hrinčár, and Peter Hrinčár. The game was published by Slovak game developer Beat Games and was later obtained by Oculus Studios. It takes place in many different surrealistic neon environments and features the player slicing blocks representing musical beats with a pair of brightly-colored sabers. Following an early access release in May 2018, the game was officially released for PlayStation 4 and Windows (supporting both Steam VR and Oculus VR APIs) on May 21, 2019, and for the Meta Quest (Oculus Quest) in standalone mode.

Beat Saber System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7/8.1/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4590 or AMD FX 8350
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 960 or AMD Radeon R9 290
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 200 MB available space
  • VR Headset: HTC Vive, Oculus Rift, or Windows Mixed Reality

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7 Skylake or equivalent
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GTX 1060 or AMD Radeon RX 480
  • DirectX: 200 MB available space
  • Storage: HTC Vive, Oculus Rift, or Windows Mixed Reality

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 4
  • PlayStation 5
  • PlayStation VR
  • PlayStation VR2
