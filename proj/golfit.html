About the Game

Golf It! is a multiplayer mini-golf game focused on fun, creativity, and challenging courses. Developed by Perfuse Entertainment, the game offers highly detailed and interactive mini-golf maps, along with a built-in level editor that allows players to design and share their own courses.

Golf It! System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 or higher (64-bit)
  • Processor: 2 GHz or better
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: 1024 MB VRAM
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 15 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-3570K / AMD Ryzen 5 1400
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GTX 780 or better
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 15 GB available space

Console & Mobile Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
