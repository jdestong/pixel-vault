About the Game

BlazBlue: Calamity Trigger is a visually stunning 2D fighting game that introduces a unique cast of characters, each with their own distinct playstyle and background. Known for its fast-paced combat and intricate mechanics, the game features the "Drive" system, allowing each fighter to unleash powerful and unique attacks based on their individual abilities. The game also boasts a deep story mode, with an intriguing narrative that immerses players into the world of BlazBlue. With its exceptional animation, tight controls, and strategic depth, BlazBlue: Calamity Trigger set a new standard in the fighting game genre.

BlazBlue: Calamity Trigger System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows XP / Vista / 7
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo 2.4 GHz or AMD Athlon 64 X2 2.4 GHz
  • Memory: 1 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce 8600 GT or ATI Radeon HD 2400
  • Storage: 4 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7 / 8
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2400 or AMD FX-6300
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 460 or AMD Radeon HD 6850
  • Storage: 5 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 3 & Xbox 360:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 4 GB of storage space for installation
