About the Game

Wuthering Waves is a story-rich open-world action RPG with a high degree of freedom. You wake from your slumber as Rover, joined by a vibrant cast of Resonators on a journey to reclaim your lost memories and surmount the Lament.

Wuthering Waves Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 64bit or Windows 11 64bit
  • Processor: intel i5 (9th Gen) / Ryzen 2700
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: GTX 1060 / RX 570
  • Storage: 30 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 64bit (DirectX 11 equivalent)
  • Processor: intel i7 (9th Gen) / Ryzen 3700
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: GTX 2060 / RX 5700XT and Above
  • Storage: 30 GB available space

Mobile Requirements

Android

  • CPU: Snapdragon Gen1+/Gen2/Gen3 processor or MediaTek processor of equivalent performance
  • RAM: 8 GB and above
  • Recommended system: System version 7.0 and above
  • Storage: At least 12 GB

IOS

  • iPhone 13 and above models; iPad with A15 SoC or processor of equivalent performance.
  • Supported system: iOS 15 and above operating system
  • Storage: At least 12 GB
