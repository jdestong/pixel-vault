About the Game

Soul Calibur VI revives the legendary weapon-based fighting series with enhanced visuals, refined mechanics, and a gripping retelling of the original Soul Calibur storyline. Featuring a diverse roster of returning and new characters, the game introduces the innovative Reversal Edge system, allowing players to counter attacks dynamically. With deep character customization, engaging single-player modes, and smooth online battles, Soul Calibur VI delivers a thrilling experience for both casual players and competitive fighters.

Soul Calibur VI System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7/8.1/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-4160 or equivalent
  • Memory: 6 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050 or AMD Radeon R9 280
  • Storage: 20 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4690 or AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 or AMD Radeon RX 480
  • Storage: 20 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 20 GB of storage space for installation

PlayStation 5 & Xbox Series X/S:

  • Backward compatible with improved performance
  • Minimum 20 GB of storage space
