About the Game

NBA 2K18 is a basketball simulation video game developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports. Released in September 2017, it is the 19th installment in the NBA 2K series and features Kyrie Irving on the cover (originally in a Cleveland Cavaliers jersey, later changed to Boston Celtics after his trade).

NBA 2K18 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7/8.1/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-530 / AMD Phenom II X4 805
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GT 450 / AMD Radeon HD 7770
  • Storage: 70 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7/8.1/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4430 / AMD FX-8370
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 770 / AMD Radeon R9 270
  • Storage: 70 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 3 & Xbox 360:

  • PS3 / Xbox 360: At least 7 GB of free space
  • PS4 / Xbox One: Around 50 GB of free space

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Nintendo Switch: 22.9 GB (digital version)
  • Required for online modes, roster updates, and MyCareer mode
