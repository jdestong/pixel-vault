About the Game

A real boxing game is a sports simulation that aims to replicate the experience of professional boxing. It features realistic physics, detailed character animations, and strategic gameplay that mimics real-life boxing techniques such as jabs, hooks, uppercuts, and defensive maneuvers like blocking and dodging.

Real Boxing System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • Operating System: Windows XP
  • Processor: AMD Athlon 64 3800+ 2.4 GHz or Intel Core2Duo 2.0 GHz
  • Memory: 3 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA 9800GT 512 MB or ATI Radeon HD 4850 512 MB
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 2 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-3570K / AMD Ryzen 5 1400
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 / AMD Radeon R9 290
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 56 GB available space (SSD recommended)

Console & Mobile Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
