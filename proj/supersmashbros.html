About the Game

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is the ultimate crossover fighting game, featuring a massive roster of over 80 playable characters from a wide range of gaming franchises. The game brings back classic stages, new mechanics like the "Final Smash" and "Spirits" systems, and introduces new fighters to keep the experience fresh. Whether playing locally with friends or competing online, Super Smash Bros. Ultimate offers a fast-paced and highly competitive multiplayer experience. The game also features a deep single-player mode with a captivating story, making it a must-have for fans of fighting games.

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate System Requirements

Switch Requirements

  • Requires Nintendo Switch console
  • Joy-Con or Pro Controller required for multiplayer
  • Minimum 16 GB of available storage for installation
  • Additional space required for downloadable content (DLC)

Online Multiplayer

Requires Nintendo Switch Online subscription for online multiplayer.
