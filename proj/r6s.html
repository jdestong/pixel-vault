About the Game

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege is a tactical first-person shooter developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft. Focused on strategic team-based gameplay, Siege features intense 5v5 matches where players take on the roles of specialized operators, each with unique gadgets and abilities. The game emphasizes destruction, tactical planning, and coordination, offering a variety of maps, game modes, and continuous updates with new content. With its highly competitive nature, Siege is a staple in the world of esports and tactical shooters.

Rainbow Six Siege System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-560 / AMD Phenom II X4 945
  • Memory: 6 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 460 / AMD Radeon HD 5870
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 85 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2500K / AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 / AMD Radeon R9 290X
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 85 GB available space (SSD recommended)

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Available on PC via Steam & Ubisoft Connect
