About the Game

EA Sports FC 25, released on September 27, 2024, is the latest installment in EA Sports' football simulation series, succeeding the FIFA franchise. This edition introduces several new features and enhancements to elevate the gaming experience.

FIFA FC25 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • Operating System: Windows 10 - 64-Bit (Latest Update)
  • Processor: AMD Ryzen 5 1600 or Intel Core i5-6600K
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics Card: AMD Radeon RX 570 or NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050 Ti
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 100 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-3570K / AMD Ryzen 5 1400
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 / AMD Radeon R9 290
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 56 GB available space (SSD recommended)

Console & Mobile Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
