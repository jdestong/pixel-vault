About the Game

Tekken 8 brings back the action-packed, fast-paced fighting mechanics the series is known for, along with new characters, arenas, and updated visuals. Players can expect enhanced graphics, more fluid animations, and an expanded roster that includes both iconic and new fighters. The game introduces new battle systems, including devastating Rage Art mechanics and the Heat System, providing a fresh experience while maintaining the spirit of Tekken's combat style.

Tekken 8 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4690 or AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 or AMD Radeon RX 580
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-7700 or AMD Ryzen 7 2700
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2070 or AMD Radeon RX 5700
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 5 & Xbox Series X/S:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 60 GB of storage space for installation

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 60 GB of hard drive space
