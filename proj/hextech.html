About the Game

Hextech Mayhem: A League of Legends Story is a rhythm platformer game developed by Choice Provisions and published by Riot Forge. It was released on November 16, 2021, for Microsoft Windows, Nintendo Switch, iOS, and Android. In this fast-paced rhythm runner, you take on the role of Ziggs, a yordle and Hexplosives expert, as you rampage through the neighborhoods of Piltover. The gameplay involves bombing, bouncing, and bopping to the beat of the music to avoid obstacles, disarm enemies, and light fuses to achieve maximum chaos. The game features a Freestyle Mayhem System that allows you to create spontaneous blasts and grab bonus points.
