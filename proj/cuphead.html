About the Game

Cuphead is a run-and-gun action game developed and published by Studio MDHR. Inspired by 1930s cartoons, the game's visuals and audio are painstakingly created using the same techniques of the era, including traditional hand-drawn animation, watercolor backgrounds, and original jazz recordings. Players control Cuphead or Mugman as they battle through various levels and bosses to repay a debt to the devil. The game is known for its challenging difficulty, retro aesthetics, and unique artistic style.

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo E8400
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: GeForce 9600 GT or equivalent
  • DirectX: Version 9.0
  • Storage: 20 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10
  • Processor: Intel i3
  • Memory: 3 GB RAM
  • Graphics: GeForce 9600 GT or equivalent
  • DirectX: Version 9.0
  • Storage: 20 GB available space

Console Availability

  • Nintendo Switch
  • PlayStation 4
  • Xbox One
  • Available on PC via Steam, GOG
