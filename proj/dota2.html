About the Game

"Dota 2" is a renowned multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Valve Corporation. In this game, two teams of five players each select from a roster of over 120 unique heroes, each with distinct abilities and roles, to compete on a map known as the Radiant and Dire sides. The main objective is to destroy the opposing team's Ancient, a crucial structure located within their base. "Dota 2" emphasizes strategic depth, skillful play, and teamwork, creating a highly competitive environment. With its deep mechanics, regular updates, and a vibrant esports scene, "Dota 2" has garnered a dedicated global player base and remains a staple in the world of competitive gaming.

Dota 2 Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 or newer
  • Processor: Dual core from Intel or AMD at 2.8 GHz
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: nVidia GeForce 8600/9600GT, ATI/AMD Radeon HD2600/3600
  • DirectX: Version 11.0
  • Storage: 60 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 or greater
  • Processor: Core i7 or AMD Ryzen 7
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: 3050Ti or greate
  • DirectX: Version 11.0
  • Storage: 60 GB available space
