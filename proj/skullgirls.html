About the Game

Skullgirls is a fast-paced, action-packed 2D fighting game with a unique cast of characters and an engaging story. Known for its fluid animations and deep combat mechanics, Skullgirls offers a rich fighting experience with a variety of game modes, including Story Mode, Arcade Mode, and multiplayer options. The game features tag-team combat, allowing players to control a team of fighters and strategize with a wide array of combos, supers, and special moves. With its unique art style, challenging gameplay, and tight controls, Skullgirls remains a beloved title for both casual and competitive players alike.

Skullgirls System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7
  • Processor: 2.0 GHz Dual Core Processor
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: ATI 4850 / Nvidia 9800GT / Intel HD 4000
  • Storage: 2 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10
  • Processor: 2.5 GHz Dual Core Processor
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: ATI 6870 / Nvidia GTX 460
  • Storage: 2 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 5 GB of storage space for installation

PlayStation 3 & Xbox 360:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 5 GB of hard drive space
