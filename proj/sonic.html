About the Game

Sonic X Shadow Generations is an action-platformer game developed by Sonic Team and published by Sega. This game masterfully combines the fast-paced thrill of classic Sonic gameplay with innovative levels featuring Shadow the Hedgehog. With remastered content from Sonic Generations and a brand-new campaign showcasing Shadow’s unique abilities, it’s a nostalgic yet fresh experience. Players can expect vibrant graphics, dynamic gameplay, and engaging challenges that redefine Shadow’s legacy.

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-4340 / AMD FX-6300
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GeForce GTX 660 / AMD Radeon HD 7950
  • Storage: 30 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2500k / AMD Ryzen 5 1600X
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GeForce GTX 970 / AMD R9 390
  • Storage: 30 GB available space

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 5
  • PlayStation 4
  • Xbox Series X/S
  • Xbox One
  • Nintendo Switch
  • Available on PC via Steam, Epic Games Store
