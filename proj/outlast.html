About the Game

Outlast is a first-person survival horror game developed and published by Red Barrels. Players take on the role of investigative journalist Miles Upshur, who ventures into the abandoned Mount Massive Asylum, only to uncover horrifying experiments and sinister secrets. The game is known for its intense atmosphere, stealth-based gameplay, and terrifying enemies. With no combat mechanics, players must rely on hiding, running, and using a night-vision camera to survive the horrors lurking within the asylum.

Outlast System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows XP / Vista / 7 / 8 / 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo E8200 / AMD Athlon 64 X2 6400+
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce 9800GTX / AMD Radeon HD 3xxx Series
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 5 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7 / 8 / 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5 / AMD FX 8100
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 460 / AMD Radeon HD 6850
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 5 GB available space
