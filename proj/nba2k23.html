About the Game

NBA 2K23 is a basketball simulation video game developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports. It is part of the NBA 2K series and was released on September 10, 2021, for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, Nintendo Switch, and PC.

NBA 2K23 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 64-bit, Windows 8.1 64-bit, or Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-2100 / AMD FX-4100
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GT 450 1GB / AMD Radeon HD 7770 1GB
  • Graphics: 128 MB video memory; ATI Radeon HD 2400 or NVIDIA 6800 GT
  • Storage: 110 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: 64-bit Windows Vista, Windows 7, or Windows 8
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4430 / AMD FX-8370
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 770 2GB / AMD Radeon R9 270 2GB
  • Storage: 110 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 3 & Xbox 360:

  • Nintendo Switch: Varies (~45 GB), requires microSD card for extra storage
  • Needed for online multiplayer, MyCareer, MyTeam, and updates

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Offline play is available for quick games and MyLeague
  • Minimum 60 GB of hard drive space
