About the Game

Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 2 is a skateboarding video game released in 2000 for PlayStation, later ported to other platforms like PC, Nintendo 64, Dreamcast, and Game Boy Advance. It builds upon the original Tony Hawk's Pro Skater, featuring improved graphics, expanded tricks (including the manual, which allows for combo extensions), and a deeper career mode.

Tony Hawk's Pro Skater 2 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 or higher (64-bit)
  • Processor: 2 GHz or better
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: 1024 MB VRAM
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 15 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-3570K / AMD Ryzen 5 1400
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GTX 780 or better
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 15 GB available space

Console & Mobile Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
