About the Game

Stick Fight: The Game is a fast-paced, physics-based fighting game where players control stick figures in a variety of environments. The game offers simple yet engaging combat, with players utilizing a variety of weapons, including guns, melee weapons, and explosives, to defeat opponents. With its hilarious ragdoll physics and unpredictable action, Stick Fight delivers an entertaining experience perfect for local multiplayer. The game supports up to 4 players, providing endless fun through chaotic battles and a range of wacky levels. It's a lighthearted and easy-to-pick-up game, making it ideal for casual gaming sessions with friends.

Stick Fight: The Game System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows XP, Vista, 7, 8, 10
  • Processor: 1.8 GHz Dual Core Processor
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: 512 MB Video Memory, Shader Model 3.0+
  • Storage: 500 MB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7, 8, 10
  • Processor: 2.0 GHz Quad Core Processor
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: 1 GB Video Memory, Shader Model 3.0+
  • Storage: 500 MB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 500 MB of storage space for installation

Switch:

  • Requires Nintendo Switch console
  • Minimum 500 MB of storage space for installation
