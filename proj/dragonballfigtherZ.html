About the Game

Dragon Ball FighterZ is a fast-paced 2.5D fighting game developed by Arc System Works and published by Bandai Namco. Featuring stunning anime visuals, tight controls, and an accessible yet deep combat system, the game brings the explosive battles of the Dragon Ball universe to life. Players can form teams of three fighters and engage in high-speed battles with iconic characters such as Goku, Vegeta, Frieza, and many more. With an immersive story mode, online multiplayer, and competitive eSports scene, Dragon Ball FighterZ delivers an electrifying experience for both casual and hardcore fighting game fans.

Dragon Ball FighterZ System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7/8/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: AMD FX-4350 / Intel Core i5-3470
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Radeon HD 6870 / GeForce GTX 650 Ti
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 6 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: AMD Ryzen 5 1400 / Intel Core i7-3770
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Radeon HD 7870 / GeForce GTX 660
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 6 GB available space

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 4
  • Xbox One
  • Nintendo Switch
