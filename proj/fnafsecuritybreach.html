About the Game

Five Nights at Freddy’s: Security Breach is a survival horror game developed by Steel Wool Studios and published by ScottGames. Set inside the massive Freddy Fazbear’s Mega Pizzaplex, players take on the role of Gregory, a young boy trapped overnight in the facility. With the help of Freddy Fazbear, Gregory must evade relentless animatronics, solve puzzles, and uncover the dark secrets of the Pizzaplex. Featuring free-roam gameplay, stealth mechanics, and an immersive horror atmosphere, Security Breach brings a fresh and thrilling experience to the FNaF series.

FNaF: Security Breach System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: AMD Ryzen 5 2600 / Intel Core i5-8600K
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050 Ti / AMD Radeon RX 590
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 80 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: AMD Ryzen 7 2700X / Intel Core i7-8700
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2060 / AMD Radeon RX 5700
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 80 GB available space (SSD recommended)

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
  • Available on PC via Steam & Epic Games Store
