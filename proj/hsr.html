About the Game

Honkai: Star Rail is a space fantasy RPG title with a journey through immense worlds of the unknown. The game features fantasy elements with myths and legends integrated into the space sci-fi story. Combined with the intuitive turn-based combat system, large maps with maze exploration, and immersive storylines together compose an interstellar melody filled with surprises and rewarding experiences, that echo throughout the universe.

Honkai Star Rail System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 SP1 64-bit, Windows 8.1 64-bit or Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel® Core™ i3 Processor
  • Memory: 6 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GeForce GTX 650 or higher
  • Storage: Please reserve at least 20 GB of storage space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows Windows 10 with 64-bit processor or higher
  • Processor: Intel® Core™ i7 Processor
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GeForce GTX 1060 or higher
  • Storage: Please reserve 30 GB of spac

Mobile Requirements

Android

  • CPU: Snapdragon 835, Dimensity 720, Kirin 810, or higher processors.
  • RAM: 4 GB and above
  • Recommended system: Android 8 or higher
  • Storage: Please reserve 8.5 GB of space

IOS

  • Phone 8 Plus and higher models. iPad with A10 processor or higher
  • Supported system: iOS 10.0 or higher
  • Storage: Please reserve 9 GB of space
