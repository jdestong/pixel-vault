About the Game

Need for Speed: Underground is a racing video game released in 2003 by EA Games, marking a major shift in the Need for Speed series towards street racing culture. The game features a deep car customization system, allowing players to modify their vehicles' performance and appearance with aftermarket parts, neon lights, vinyls, and body kits.

NFS Underground System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 98/ME/2000/XP
  • Processor: Pentium III or AMD Athlon 700 MHz
  • RAM: 128 MB
  • Graphics: 32 MB DirectX 9.0c compatible video card
  • Storage: 2 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: 64-bit Windows Vista, Windows 7, or Windows 8
  • Processor: Pentium III or AMD Athlon 1.5 GHz
  • Memory: 2 GB RAM
  • Graphics: 64 MB DirectX 9.0c compatible video card
  • Storage: 2 GB available space

Console Requirements

A PlayStation 2 console & A PS2-compatible controller

  • Requires respective console
  • Ensure sufficient storage space for installation and updates
