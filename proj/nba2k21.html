About the Game

NBA 2K21 is a basketball simulation video game developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports. It is the 21st installment in the NBA 2K series and features Anthony Davis on the Standard Edition cover, while Dwyane Wade appears on the Legend Edition.

NBA 2K21 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 (64-bit) or later
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-530 / AMD FX-4100 (or better)
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GT 450 / AMD Radeon HD 7770 (or better)
  • Storage: 80 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7 (64-bit) or later
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4430 / AMD FX-8370 (or better)
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 770 / AMD Radeon R9 270 (or better)
  • Storage: 80 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 3 & Xbox 360:

  • PS4 / Xbox One: ~50 GB free space
  • Nintendo Switch: ~32 GB free space (microSD recommended for digital version)

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Required for online features like MyCareer, MyTeam, and roster updates.
  • Offline modes like Play Now and MyLeague are available.
