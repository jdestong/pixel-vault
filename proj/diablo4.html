About the Game

Diablo IV is an action role-playing game developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment. It is the fourth main installment in the Diablo series and was released on June 5, 2023. Players create a character from one of six playable classes—Barbarian, Druid, Necromancer, Rogue, Sorcerer, or Spiritborn—and use their skills to complete quests through combat. The game features procedurally generated dungeons, loot-focused character-building, and an open world.

Assassin's Creed: Brotherhood System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit (Version 1909 or newer)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2500K or AMD™ FX-8350
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 or AMD Radeon R9 280
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: Solid State Drive (SSD) with 90GB available space
  • Internet: Broadband internet connection

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit (Version 1909 or newer)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4670K or AMD R3-1300X
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 or AMD Radeon RX 470
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: Solid State Drive (SSD) with 90GB available space
  • Internet: Broadband internet connection

Console Availability

  • Windows PC (Battle.net and Steam)
  • PlayStation 4
  • PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One
  • Xbox Series X|S
