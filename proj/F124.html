About the Game

F1 24 is the official video game of the 2024 FIA Formula One World Championship, developed by Codemasters and published by EA Sports. Released on May 31, 2024, for platforms including Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S, the game offers an immersive Formula One racing experience.

F1 24 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7 or higher (64-bit)
  • Processor: 2 GHz or better
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: 1024 MB VRAM
  • DirectX: Version 9.0c
  • Storage: 15 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-3570K / AMD Ryzen 5 1400
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GTX 780 or better
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 15 GB available space

Console & Mobile Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
  • Nintendo Switch
