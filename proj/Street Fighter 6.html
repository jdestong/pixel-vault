About the Game

Street Fighter 6 delivers an epic, action-packed fighting experience with a fresh take on the classic series. Featuring new and returning fighters, updated graphics, and dynamic new stages, this entry pushes the limits of the franchise's signature fighting mechanics. The game introduces the innovative "Drive System," allowing players to customize their fighting style with a variety of new moves and strategies. With revamped online modes, improved visuals, and exciting new mechanics, Street Fighter 6 is set to bring the ultimate fighting experience to players worldwide.

Street Fighter 6 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-4690 or AMD Ryzen 5 1600
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 or AMD Radeon RX 580
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-7700 or AMD Ryzen 7 2700
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2070 or AMD Radeon RX 5700
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 5 & Xbox Series X/S:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 60 GB of storage space for installation

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • Requires respective console
  • Minimum 60 GB of hard drive space
