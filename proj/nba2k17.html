About the Game

NBA 2K17 is a basketball simulation video game developed by Visual Concepts and published by 2K Sports. Released in September 2016, it is the 18th installment in the NBA 2K series. The game aims to deliver a realistic NBA experience with enhanced gameplay mechanics, improved graphics, and deeper career and franchise modes.

NBA 2K17 System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7/8.1/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo or AMD Athlon X2 (2.4 GHz or better)
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: DirectX 10.1 compatible (512 MB)
  • Storage: 70 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 7/8.1/10 (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5 or AMD FX (2.7 GHz or better)
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: DirectX 11 compatible (2 GB or more)
  • Storage: 70 GB available space

Console Requirements

PlayStation 3 & Xbox 360:

  • Required for online modes, roster updates, and MyCareer mode
  • Offline play is available with limited features

PlayStation 4 & Xbox One:

  • PS3 / Xbox 360: Around 10 GB free space
  • PS4 / Xbox One / Switch: Around 70 GB free space
