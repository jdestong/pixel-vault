For Honor System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: Windows 7/8.1/10 (64-bit only)
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-550 / AMD Phenom II X4 955
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 / AMD Radeon HD 6970 (2 GB VRAM)
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Network: Broadband Internet connection
  • Storage: 40 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit only)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2500K / AMD FX-6350
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 770 / AMD Radeon R9 280X (3 GB VRAM)
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Network: Broadband Internet connection
  • Storage: 40 GB available space

Console Availability

  • PlayStation 4 & PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One & Xbox Series X/S
