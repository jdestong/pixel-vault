About the Game

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is an action role-playing game developed and published by CD Projekt Red. Released on May 19, 2015, for PlayStation 4, Windows, and Xbox One, it later became available on Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X/S. The game is the third installment in The Witcher series, based on the fantasy novels by Andrzej Sapkowski. In The Witcher 3, players control Geralt of Rivia, a monster slayer known as a Witcher, as he searches for his adopted daughter, Ciri, who is on the run from the otherworldly Wild Hunt. The game features an open world with a third-person perspective, allowing players to explore various regions, interact with non-player characters, and complete quests to gain experience points and gold. The story has three possible endings, determined by the player's choices at key points in the narrative.

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt System Requirements

PC Requirements

Minimum:

  • OS: 64-bit Windows 7 or 64-bit Windows 8 (8.1)
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2500K 3.3GHz or AMD A10-5800K APU (3.8GHz)
  • Memory: 6 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GeForce GTX 660 or AMD Radeon HD 7870
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Recommended:

  • OS: 64-bit Windows 7 or 64-bit Windows 8 (8.1) (64-bit)
  • Processor: Intel Core i7 3770 3.4GHz or AMD FX-8350 4GHz
  • Memory: 6 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GeForce GTX 770 or AMD Radeon R9 290
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Storage: 50 GB available space

Console Availability

  • Nintendo Switch
  • PlayStation 4
  • PlayStation 5
  • Xbox One
  • Xbox Series X/S
